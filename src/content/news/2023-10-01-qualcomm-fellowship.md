I received the **2023 Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship - North America**.

The Qualcomm Innovation Fellowship (QIF) supports PhD students pursuing research in mobile and related technologies. Winners receive a fellowship grant and mentorship from Qualcomm engineers.

My fellowship proposal focused on research in unary computing for energy-efficient AI accelerators.
