Our paper **"Catwalk: Unary Top-K for Efficient Ramp-No-Leak Neuron Design for Temporal Neural Networks"** received the **Amar Mukherjee Best Paper Award** at ISVLSI 2025.

This work, co-authored with Drew Lister, Prof. John Paul Shen, and Prof. Di Wu, presents a unary-based Top-K implementation for Temporal Neural Networks.

The Amar Mukherjee Best Paper Award is presented at the IEEE Computer Society Annual Symposium on VLSI.
